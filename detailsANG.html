A little more about us

Hotel Project - a legendary Hotel in Warsaw A Five star Project is without a doubt the best, lendary luxurious hotel in Warsaw. It lays in a beautiful building in the old part of town. You can feel the authentic 100-year old tradition as soon as you step foot inside, thanks to the historical floors, exposed under a thich glass in the main hall. The period of the highest prosperity of the Project was in the XX century, when the hotel was a favorite meeting place of industry owners, businessmen, tourists and local artists. The inside brings you back to feeling like in the early 30's of the last century. However, the style harmoincally connects to a modern interior through subtle decoration, magnificent materials, exotic wood, oriental rugs, cotton and marble. All of this brilliantly clicks together to create a unique impression. The interiors have a very bold design, and are accompanied by artistic, impressive fotograms, mostly black and white. All of this connects to allow for an experience unlike any other hotel - you can feel like you've traveled in time in order to witness the kind of Silesia that can no longer be found anywhere. All of the rooms are well equipped and have AC. In larger areas and open rooms, and also in all rooms in general, you can access a free wireless internet connection (Wi-Fi). The guests can freely use; a stylish swimming pool, two saunas, and a gym. In Project we have two bars, two restaurants; polish and italian equipped with a luxurious kitchen. For companies interested in organizing business meetings, we have three conference rooms available. Included with a conference; coffee breaks, lunches, dinners. We can also prepare a wedding, banquets, auctions and shows. We encourage you to step into our shopping mall inside the Hotel.
